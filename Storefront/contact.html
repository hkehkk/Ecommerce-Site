Hannah has been crafting for more than 10 years. She loves to keep incorporate new ideas into her products. She pays very close attention to detail, you will know your piece was created with perfection. Hannah adds new products to her website weekly. You will likely see new creations each time.

Hannah has joined multiple crafting and business groups to keep her small business running smoothly. With the help of other minds, Hannah was able to open her own business in 2021. Currently, you can find her work on this site and at craft shows around Montana. Hannah hopes to have a store front of her own one day.

Contact Hannah, by email, to create a custom piece for yourself or another. Hannah will respond within 48 hours to your inquiry. She would like to thank you for visiting her sight and considering her products.
